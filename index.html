Redefining Durian Farming

The Durian as a mysterios fruit with foul smelling scent but enchanting taste is the best gift god has given to tropical forest. However there are much more knowledges than you could expect in the fruit farming and grading itself.

We who aim to produce best Durians have the best team in assuring the quality of our production. Over the decade, a special grading system to define the fruit has been calibrated. We promise to sell only the best fruit to our customers.

Our farming site is located at a small peak in the town of Kuala Lipis. The nutritious soil and specialised traditional, manual way of farming ensured each fruit from our farm is a product of quality.

Our organisation is widely recognised nation-wide, the top-notch fruit that we harvest and the quality assured processing is almost unbeatable in the industry. We often see ourselves as producer of not only food to eat but also gift to give.
